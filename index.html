about me

I have a PhD in Philosophy from the University of Buenos Aires. I specialize in philosophy of language. I'm an Assistant Researcher at the National Scientific and Technical Research Council (CONICET) and JTP (chief of practical assignments) at the Department of Philosophy of the University of Buenos Aires (Faculty of Arts and Science). I'm also Junior Researcher at the Buenos Aires Logic Group. Currently I'm working on deontic and epistemic modals, and on expressive and honorific content. Recently, I've done work on truth relativism, and I am still dealing from some remnants of that discussion. Other areas that interest me are theories of aboutness, the semantics of aesthetic predicates, and not-at-issue content.
