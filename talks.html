Places I've been thanks to philosophy

Here you will find some of my talks.

You'll notice that I've traveled mostly within Argentina and neighboring countries. It is hard for scholars of developing countries to have access to funding for travel purposes, and when it is available, it is hardly enough. So, if you happen be reading this and you are planing a workshop, give hybrid modality a chance, keeping things strictly face-to-face simply silences more than half the world!
